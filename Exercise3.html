How To Create Your Own Ethernet Cable

Creating your own ethernet cable is easy and fun. In this list, I will teach you how to create your own ethernet cable in simple steps.

These are the things you need:

  1. Unshielded Twisted Pair (UTP) Patch Cable
  2. Modular Connector (8P8C plug, aka RJ45)
  3. Crimping Tool
    1. (Optional) Cable Tester
    2. (Optional) Cable Stripper

Instructions:

  1. Strip the cable jacket in both ends at about 1.5 inches down from the end.
  2. Four (4) cables should be seen when spread. Untwist all the wires.
  3. Align all the wires in one end in this orientation from left to right:

      • White/Green
      • Green
      • White/Orange
      • Blue
      • White/Blue
      • Orange
      • White/Brown
      • Brown
      • White/Orange
      • Orange
      • White/Green
      • Blue
      • White/Blue
      • Green
      • White/Brown
      • Brown

  4. Take your RJ45. While looking on the gold plated pins, slide your aligned wires into the pins. Slide them until they are firmly secured.
  5. Without letting go, take your crimping tool. Slide the RJ45 with the wires in the slot of the crimping tool and crimp firmly.
  6. As an optional step, you may use a cable tester to test if your cables are successfully crimped.
